TechCon began in 2018 as a small, regional meetup for software developers passionate about open source technology. It quickly grew into a major international conference, driven by the need for a dedicated space where innovators could share cutting-edge research and ethical considerations for future tech.

Today, TechCon hosts over 10,000 attendees annually, solidifying its position as a leading voice in the global tech community.
